About Us

We are Indika, a geography teacher, and my husband, a civil engineer. Together with our three teenage children, we've opened our architect-designed home to guests who want to experience the beauty and calm of Sri Lanka.

Nestled in a peaceful village just 5 minutes from Anuradhapura's city centre, our villa is surrounded by a lush garden of heritage fruit trees and organic vegetables that attract colorful birds and wildlife.
